Features & Innovations

Learn about the key features of the 2026 Toyota Camry that improve safety, efficiency, and convenience.

Toyota Safety Sense 3.0

The 2026 Camry comes with Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 (TSS), which includes a Pre-Collision System, Lane Tracing Assist, Automatic High Beams, and a Proactive Driving Assist. These features help with steering and braking, making everyday driving safer and less tiring.

5th-Gen Hybrid Powertrain

Equipped with a 5th-generation hybrid system, combining a 2.5L petrol engine and an electric motor. Offers 225 hp (FWD) or 232 hp (AWD), and around 52 mpg city / 49 mpg highway. The hybrid system is self-charging, so no plug-in is required.

12.3" Multimedia Display

Comes with a 12.3-inch touchscreen supporting wireless CarPlay and Android Auto, over-the-air updates, and a 10-speaker audio system. A head-up display projects key information like speed and navigation onto the windshield.

Redesigned Exterior

The 2026 Camry has a sharper Hammerhead front design, LED headlights, wider grille, and optional two-tone paint. Aerodynamic styling reduces drag, and 18-inch wheels complete the new look.
