Overview

The Toyota Camry has been a popular mid-size sedan worldwide since 1982. The 2026 model combines modern design, comfortable interior, and efficient hybrid options. Drivers value its reliability, fuel efficiency, and everyday usability. The hybrid version uses a 2.5-litre petrol engine paired with an electric motor for improved fuel economy and smooth performance.
